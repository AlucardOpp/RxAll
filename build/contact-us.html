CONTACT US

We’re here to raise awareness and start the conversation about counterfeit medicines and their dangers. Get in touch to learn more about our solutions and how we can partner to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

  • RxALL

    RxAll is a digital infrastructure for hospitals and pharmacies, including a drug testing platform, pharmacy management software, and an invoice financing platform.

    Management team: info@rxall.net

    Questions: info@rxall.net

  • RxDelivered

    RxDelivered connects buyers with proven sellers, offering already authentic medicines at affordable prices. All products on the marketplace are RxScanner verified.

    Management team: info@rxall.net

    Questions: rasheed.olatunji@rxall.net

    RxScanner

    The RxScanner uses AI and machine learning to check and confirm the quality of drug samples and helps FDAs, hospitals, and pharmacies to provide patients with high-quality medicines.

    Management team: info@rxall.net

    Questions: pius.alabi@rxall.net

  • RxPay

    RxPay enables pharmacies and wholesalers to get funding on soft terms for required purchases, preventing stock-outs and keeping drugs accessible without driving prices up.

    Management team: info@rxall.net

    Questions: pius.alabi@rxall.net

    RxPOS

    RxPOS is software for automating sales & purchases processes, quick payments, simple invoicing, better inventory & reordering management, and improving customer loyalty.

    Management team: info@rxall.net

    Questions: rasheed.olatunji@rxall.net

  • RxAcademy

    RxAcademy is an on-demand platform training pharmacists to open and run their pharmacy business with up to $10K in inventory & asset financing per person.

    Management team: rasheed.olatunji@rxall.net

    Questions: rasheed.olatunji@rxall.net

    RxHMO

    RxHMO provides insured customers with access to authentic drugs from accredited pharmacies nationwide on RxDelivered and credits HMOs so their customers can get their prescriptions faster without waiting.

    Management team: info@rxall.net

    Questions: pius.alabi@rxall.net

  • RxGo

    RxGo is a curated pharmacy network, offering already authenticated quality drugs and consumer products available for in-person and online ordering with delivery.

    Management team: info@rxall.net

    Questions: rasheed.olatunji@rxall.net
